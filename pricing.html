What Impacts the Cost?

Mural pricing is calculated by square footage, but these specific variables will adjust the final quote.

Level of Detail

A photorealistic portrait takes significantly more time and skill per square foot than a flat, geometric color block design.

Wall Texture

Smooth drywall is fast to paint. Heavily textured brick or corrugated metal absorbs more paint and requires tedious.

Equipment Needed

Ground-level walls are standard. Walls over 10 feet require scaffolding, scissor lifts, or boom lifts which incur rental.
