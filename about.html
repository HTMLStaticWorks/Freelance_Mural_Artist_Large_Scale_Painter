The Journey

A Passion For Scale.

I didn't start on canvases; I started on concrete walls. For over a decade, I've been obsessed with how scale and color can completely alter the emotion of a physical space.

My journey began in street art, learning the raw power of spray paint. As my style evolved, I merged that urban energy with classical fine art techniques, leading to the creation of CanvasCraft Studio.

Today, I work directly with architects, interior designers, and brands to paint murals that aren't just decorations, but architectural anchors.
