Service Profile

Corporate Offices

Elevating Workspace Culture

A boring white wall in an office doesn't inspire innovation. Corporate murals are designed to physically manifest a company's core values, history, and mission directly onto their workspace walls.

From tech startups in Silicon Valley to established law firms in New York, I design and execute bespoke murals that turn mundane offices into vibrant, energetic environments where employees want to work and clients are immediately impressed.

  • Brand Identity Integration
  • Low-VOC, Odorless Paints for Indoor Safety
  • Weekend Installation Available to Avoid Disruption

Service Specs

Typical Scale: 100 - 1,000 Sq.Ft
Timeline: 1 - 3 Weeks
Primary Medium: Acrylic & Low-VOC Spray
Starting Price: $3,500
